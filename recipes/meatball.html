Spaghetti and Meatballs!

Spaghetti with Meatballs: This dish has a rich and hearty flavor, thanks to the combination of the savory meatballs and the flavorful tomato sauce. The pasta is a good source of carbohydrates, which provide energy, and the meatballs are a good source of protein.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pot of boiling, salted water, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic, Parmesan, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined. Roll the mixture into 1-inch balls and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pan and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the meatballs are cooked through.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the sauce and meatballs and toss to combine. Garnish with basil, if desired, and serve hot.
